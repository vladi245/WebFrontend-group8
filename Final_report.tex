\section{Introduction}


\subsection{Motivation}
Prolonged sitting can harm both wellbeing and productivity, yet people often forget to stand, stretch, or take short breaks throughout the day. Additionally, the lack of integration between work routines and fitness apps makes it difficult to accurately track and assess physical activity, limiting the ability to maintain a healthy, active lifestyle.

\subsection{Objective}
Our objective is to help people keep track of their habits. The way we do this is to present an application, that with the data given from the user, GetStanding present it in a clear, easy to understand format. One of the most important objective was to get this is done through one application. GetStanding, unlike other fitness tracking applications, also has the capability to connect to a sit and stand desk, to track the users time spent standing or sitting.

\subsection{Delimitation}
Most of our problem were related to the time limitations we had. We wanted to add other features like a friends page for example but have decided that we do not have the time capacity to pull it off. Our planning also wasnt always the best, and because of this sometime some tasks were delayed making it harder to move forward with the whole project.
